A loose following of Sebastian Lague's Procedural Planet tutorial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QN39W020LqU&list=PLFt_AvWsXl0cONs3T0By4puYy6GM22ko8

I liked the icosahedron sphere better than the balloonified cube, so I searched theinternet.com on how to make an icosahedron, and figured out the resolution system myself.
Luckily Processing has its own Perlin Noise generator: https://processing.org/reference/noise_.html

For controls, look inside the keyPressed() function in both ProcPlanets and First_Person_Perspective
